S Competence

membership examination

S Competence exams are conducted for candidates for those who have a dream of starting their own business or company & seeking self employment opportunity within India. Those interested aspirants can crack the exam and avail Certification & membership with proper Funding & Support in order to setup their Dream Business. Exams are in the form of Online Aptitude Test contains Level I & Level II.

Level I is a Home Proctored Online Assessment, candidate can take the exams from their Remote Location. Those who crack Level I exams are eligible to take Final Benchmark Exam as Level II

STRATEGIC BOOTSTRAP FOR CREATIVE IDEAS & SMALL BUSINESS PROJECTS OF INDIAN YOUNG ENTREPRENUERS

"Everybody has Business Idea but nobody know right Business Pitch"

As we know India has the greatest youth population in the world. Likewise Indian economy has largest pool of Innovative & Creative young minds with potential and creative problem solving ideas for the nation. But Indian youngsters are lacking structural platform to take their Idea as passion of their life. As a result most of the time educated youngsters were forced them to do a less paying job due to their Family & Social situations. Here We should accept the ground fact i.e.

'In India youngsters are employed but most cases they are Under-Employed'

As a business accelerator S Competence has a vision to empower Indian youths & women by creating self employment opportunity and also building a structural ecosystem to enable their ideas into reality with the help of Strategic Mentorship & Financial Support System Protocol (SMFSSP). We take it has our primary mission to boost self confidence of youths having with or without business Ideas to became a successful Entrepreneur as well as Founders or co founders of the Startups in India irrespective of their Gender, Geography, Cast, Religion, Education, Ethnicity and Society.
